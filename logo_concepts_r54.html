LOGO CONCEPTS - ROUND 54

The Soft Kinetic Series (Curves & Flow)

Option 231: Soft Standard

Refinement: Rounded Caps. The sharp "cut" ends are replaced with full semi-circle caps, making the form friendly and continuous.

Option 232: True Radius

Refinement: Pipe Bends. The corners are bent pipes (True Radius) rather than just rounded joints. Smooth, flowing infrastructure.

Option 233: Liquid Flow

Refinement: Thicker S. The foundation (S) is slightly thicker and fully curved, suggesting a liquid flowing downhill.

Option 234: Tubular Tech

Refinement: 3D Tube. Highlights are added to the center of the strokes, creating a cylindrical, neon-tube effect.

Option 235: Extended Flow

Refinement: Long Tails. The "Start" and "End" strokes are elongated, exaggerating the kinetic motion.

Option 236: The Racer (Soft)

Refinement: Rounded Inline. The "Speed Stripe" concept adapted to the soft geometry. Sporty and friendly.

Option 237: Aero Curve

Refinement: Wing Profile. The leading beam (G Roof) has a teardrop/airfoil shape, blending curve and point.

Option 238: Neon Stream

Refinement: Vapor Flow. Purple/Pink gradients on true-radius curves. High energy and fluid.

Option 239: Gold Stream

Refinement: Liquid Gold. Rich metallic gradient on the smooth forms. Luxurious and modern.

Option 240: Hydro Dynamic

Refinement: Bulbous Ends. The stroke terminals are capped with circles, giving a water-droplet effect at the start and end.
