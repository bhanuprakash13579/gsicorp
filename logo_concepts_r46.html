LOGO CONCEPTS - ROUND 46

The Integrated Series (10 Variations of Option 150)

Option 151: Direct Contact

Variation: Zero Gap. The S lines physically touch the G area. Highly geometric, brick-like locking mechanism.

Option 152: The Fused Beam

Variation: Heavy Bars. The G-Roof and S-Floor are thickened into colored structural beams. The letters hang/stand from them.

Option 153: Heavy Industry

Variation: Solid Blocks. Ultra-heavy weight. Looks like construction equipment or stamped steel.

Option 154: The Aero Link

Variation: Rounded Speed. Smooth, rounded terminals. Italicized for motion. Friendly but tech-forward.

Option 155: Cyber Edge

Variation: Outline Tech. Thin, sharp outlines with 45-degree chamfered corners. Precision engineering look.

Option 156: Quantum Weave

Variation: Layered. The extensions and letters overlap with depth (layering order). Feels assembled.

Option 157: Stencil Block

Variation: Cargo/Shipping. Solid blocks with stencil cutouts. "No gap" in spacing, but cuts for style.

Option 158: Kinetic Slab

Variation: Directional. The extensions have arrow-heads, emphasizing speed right and speed left.

Option 159: Infinity Trace

Variation: Neon Line. Thin, continuous neon tubing style. Minimalist and very modern.

Option 160: The Monolith

Variation: Negative Space. The letterforms are cut out of a solid metal slab that follows the G-Right/S-Left geometry.
