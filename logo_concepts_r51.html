LOGO CONCEPTS - ROUND 51

Variations of Option 198 (Cyber Chamfer)

Option 201: The Base Cyber

Chamfer: Standard 45 Deg. Original Option 198 reference. Balanced cuts on the primary turns.

Option 202: Deep Chamfer

Chamfer: Radical. The cuts are much longer (80px), creating a more geometric, stealth-fighter silhouette.

Option 203: The Hex Cut

Chamfer: 60 Degree. Steeper angles that mimic hexagon/techno geometry rather than standard 45s.

Option 204: Inverted Notch

Chamfer: Internal Step. The corners step inwards (90 degree notches) instead of smoothing out.

Option 205: The Segmented

Chamfer: Broken Joint. The cuts act as break points, separating the logo into modular plates.

Option 206: The Dual Cut

Chamfer: Gem Facet. Double-angle cuts on the turns, creating a more complex, jewel-like faceting.

Option 207: The Razor Grid

Chamfer: Wireframe. Thin strokes with sharp bevels, sitting on a fine construction grid.

Option 208: The Heavy Plate

Chamfer: Armor. Ultra-thick strokes with bevels. Looks like tank plating or heavy machinery.

Option 209: The Precision Cut

Chamfer: Panel Line. A fine negative line runs through the chamfer, emphasizing the joinery.

Option 210: The Stealth Angle

Chamfer: Omni-Directional. Not just the corners, but the terminals (ends) are also diamond-cut for maximum stealth geometry.
