LOGO CONCEPTS - ROUND 48

Structural Variations of Option 150

Option 171: The Sharp Angle

Structure: Beveled Corers. Hard 45-degree cuts at the turns instead of 90-degree. Aggressive and angular.

Option 172: The Soft Turn

Structure: Full Radius. Pipe-like bends. Smooth flow, less industrial, more approachable.

Option 173: The Floating Roof

Structure: Detached. The extensions act as independent bars hovering above and below the letters.

Option 174: The Tapered Flight

Structure: Aerodynamic. The extension beams taper to a point, simulating high-speed motion trails.

Option 175: The Notched Block

Structure: Mechanical. Small square notches are added at the joints to make it look like assembled parts.

Option 176: The Speed Stripe

Structure: Inline. A "racing stripe" runs through the center of every stroke. Geometric and sporty.

Option 177: The Heavy Slab

Structure: Contrast. Vertical lines are very thick (pillars). Horizontal extensions are thin (beams).

Option 178: Tech Stencil

Structure: Stenciled cuts. The continuous lines are broken by deliberate stencil gaps, creating a modular system.

Option 179: Connected Circuit

Structure: Schematic. Thin lines ending in connection dots (Nodes). Internet of Things aesthetic.

Option 180: The Z-Lock

Structure: Angular Diagonal. The S is simplified into a Z-shaped lightning bolt structure, creating a diagonal dynamic.
